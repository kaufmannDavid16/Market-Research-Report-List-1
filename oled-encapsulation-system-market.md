<p><ul><li>TFE</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED encapsulation system market features two primary types: Thin Film Encapsulation (TFE) and hybrid encapsulation. TFE utilizes a monolithic layer of barrier materials to protect OLEDs, providing excellent moisture resistance while maintaining flexibility and thinness. In contrast, hybrid encapsulation combines organic and inorganic materials, enhancing barrier performance and durability. This method allows for improved thermal stability and compatibility with various substrates, catering to diverse applications in display and lighting technologies, ultimately driving the market's growth.</p></p>
