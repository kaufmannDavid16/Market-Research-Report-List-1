<p><p>The label adhesives market is categorized into three main types: water-based, hot melt-based, and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based adhesives utilize a blend of water and polymers, offering eco-friendliness and easy cleanup, making them ideal for various applications. Hot melt-based adhesives are thermoplastic and provide rapid bonding, making them suitable for high-speed production environments. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, delivering strong adhesion and durability, particularly for heavy-duty applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on performance requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
