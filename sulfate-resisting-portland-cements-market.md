<p><p>The sulfate-resisting Portland cement market is categorized primarily into two consistency types: standard consistency and extra thick consistency. Standard consistency is designed for typical applications, providing sufficient resistance to sulfate attacks in standard environments, making it ideal for general construction. In contrast, extra thick consistency cement is formulated for applications in harsher conditions, featuring a denser composition that enhances durability and is suited for severe sulfate exposure, such as in marine environments or infrastructure subjected to high sulfate levels.</p></p>
