PLM focuses on managing a product's entire life from conception to retirement, ensuring efficient design, development, and compliance. In contrast, SLM deals with the support and maintenance of products post-deployment, optimizing service efficiency and prolonging asset lifespan. Together, these markets enhance operational effectiveness, reduce costs, and ensure that military aerospace assets remain mission-ready throughout their lifecycle.</p></p>
