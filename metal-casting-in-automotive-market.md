<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metal Casting in Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Casting</li><li>High-Pressure Die Casting (HPDC)</li><li>Low-Pressure Die Casting (LPDC)</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal casting in the automotive market involves various techniques for producing components efficiently. Gravity casting utilizes gravitational force to pour molten metal into molds, suitable for intricate shapes with good surface finish. High-Pressure Die Casting (HPDC) injects molten metal into molds under high pressure, ideal for mass production with excellent dimensional accuracy. Low-Pressure Die Casting (LPDC) employs lower pressure for gentler filling, often used for lightweight components. Sand casting, involving sand molds, is versatile and cost-effective, suitable for lower production volumes and complex shapes.</p></p>
