<p><p>Styrene Isoprene Styrene (SIS) is a type of thermoplastic elastomer used in various applications for its flexibility and resilience. The market for SIS can be categorized into linear and radial types. Linear SIS features a straight-chain structure, suitable for applications requiring good clarity and strength, primarily in adhesives and sealants. In contrast, radial SIS has a branched structure, offering enhanced compatibility and flow properties, making it ideal for uses in tires, footwear, and other flexible materials.</p></p>
