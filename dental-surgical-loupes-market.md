<p><ul><li>Clip-On Loupe</li><li>Headband Mounted Loupe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental surgical loupes market comprises primarily two types: clip-on loupes and headband-mounted loupes. Clip-on loupes are lightweight attachments that can be affixed to regular eyewear, providing enhanced magnification without the need for specialized glasses. In contrast, headband-mounted loupes are designed to be worn directly on the head, offering stability and comfort during prolonged dental procedures. Both types enhance precision and visibility, catering to varying preferences and professional requirements within the dental field.</p></p>
