<p><ul><li>Retrofit X-ray Systems</li><li>New Digital X-ray Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital X-ray devices market comprises two main types: retrofit X-ray systems and new digital X-ray systems. Retrofit X-ray systems involve upgrading existing traditional X-ray machines with digital imaging technology, enhancing their capabilities without the need for complete replacement. These systems are cost-effective and extend the life of older equipment. In contrast, new digital X-ray systems are complete, state-of-the-art devices designed from the ground up, offering advanced imaging quality, improved workflow efficiency, and integration with modern healthcare technologies.</p></p>
