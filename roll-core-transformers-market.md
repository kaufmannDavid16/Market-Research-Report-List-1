<p><p>The Roll-core Transformers market is segmented into two primary types: Dry Type and Oil-immersed Type. Dry Type transformers utilize air as a cooling medium, making them suitable for indoor applications with limited space and safety concerns, as they pose no fire risk. Oil-immersed transformers, on the other hand, use oil for cooling and insulation, offering better thermal efficiency and more robust performance in larger installations. Each type serves distinct applications based on environmental and operational requirements.</p></p>
