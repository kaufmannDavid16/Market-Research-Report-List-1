<p><p>The airway endoscopes market consists of flexible and rigid endoscopes, each serving distinct medical purposes. Flexible endoscopes are designed for procedures requiring maneuverability, allowing for easy navigation through the airways and optimal visualization of internal structures. They are commonly used in bronchoscopies. In contrast, rigid endoscopes offer a fixed structure, providing enhanced stability and direct line-of-sight, typically used in surgeries or examinations requiring precise access. Both types are essential for diagnosing and treating respiratory conditions effectively.</p></p>
