<p><p>Microcrystalline cellulose powder is primarily categorized into two market types: refined wood pulp-based and refined cotton-based. The refined wood pulp-based type is derived from wood sources and typically offers high purity, making it suitable for various applications in food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. In contrast, the refined cotton-based type originates from cotton fibers and is favored for its superior moisture retention and stability. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing texture, acting as a bulking agent, and improving the overall quality of products.</p></p>
