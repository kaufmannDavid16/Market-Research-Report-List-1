<p><p>The 2019-nCoV testing kit market is primarily divided into two types: Fluorescent PCR and Colloidal Gold tests. Fluorescent PCR utilizes polymerase chain reaction technology to detect viral RNA with high sensitivity and specificity, making it suitable for accurate diagnostic testing. Colloidal Gold tests, on the other hand, are rapid antigen tests that rely on colorimetric detection, providing quick results but generally with lower sensitivity. Both types serve distinct roles in COVID-19 diagnostics, catering to different clinical needs and testing scenarios.</p></p>
