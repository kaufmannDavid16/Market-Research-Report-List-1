<p><p>The pregnancy test meters market is divided into two main types: urine test meters and blood test meters. Urine test meters are portable devices that detect pregnancy hormones in urine, offering quick and convenient results, making them popular for home use. Blood test meters, on the other hand, are used in clinical settings and provide more accurate results by measuring hormone levels in blood samples. Both types cater to different needs, with urine tests prioritizing convenience and blood tests focusing on reliability.</p></p>
