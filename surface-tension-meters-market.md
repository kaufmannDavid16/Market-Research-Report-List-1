<p><ul><li>Static Surface Tension Meter</li><li>Dynamic Surface Tension Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surface tension meters market is categorized into two main types: static surface tension meters and dynamic surface tension meters. Static surface tension meters measure the surface tension of liquids at rest, providing accurate values for surface properties under stable conditions. In contrast, dynamic surface tension meters assess surface tension during the process of change, such as when a liquid is actively agitated or when bubbles are forming. Both types are essential for various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and petrochemicals, to ensure quality and performance.</p></p>
