<p><ul><li>Cone Types</li><li>Dome Types</li><li>Compression Horn Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The midrange speaker market is categorized mainly into three types: cone, dome, and compression horn speakers. Cone midrange speakers feature a conical diaphragm for wide dispersion and efficient sound reproduction, suitable for home audio systems. Dome midrange speakers utilize a dome-shaped diaphragm for enhanced clarity and a smooth frequency response, often used in high-fidelity applications. Compression horn midrange speakers combine a compression driver with a horn to deliver high output and focused sound, primarily used in professional audio and PA systems. These types cater to diverse listening needs and environments.</p></p>
