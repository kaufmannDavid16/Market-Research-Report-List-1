<p><p>The gas meter market comprises wet gas meters and dry gas meters, each serving distinct applications. Wet gas meters, which use a liquid to measure gas flow, are ideal for high-accuracy requirements in residential and commercial settings. In contrast, dry gas meters employ mechanical components to measure gas flow without liquid, making them suitable for lower flow rates and industrial applications. Both types play crucial roles in energy management, billing, and monitoring gas consumption across various sectors.</p></p>
