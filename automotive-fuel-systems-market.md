<p><p>The automotive fuel systems market comprises three main types: gasoline systems, diesel oil systems, and CNG or LPG systems. Gasoline systems utilize petrol for spark-ignited engines, known for their performance and efficiency. Diesel oil systems cater to compression-ignited engines, offering superior torque and fuel economy. CNG (compressed natural gas) and LPG (liquefied petroleum gas) systems provide cleaner alternatives, reducing emissions and dependency on fossil fuels. Each system serves distinct vehicle types and consumer preferences within the evolving automotive landscape.</p></p>
