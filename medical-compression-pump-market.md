<p><p>The medical compression pump market is divided into two primary types: dynamic and static. Dynamic compression pumps employ a rhythmic motion to provide variable pressure to enhance blood circulation and lymphatic drainage, often used in conditions like lymphedema and venous issues. Static compression pumps, on the other hand, maintain a consistent pressure to support venous return and reduce swelling. Both types serve crucial roles in managing various medical conditions, offering tailored solutions to improve patient outcomes in rehabilitation and post-operative care.</p></p>
