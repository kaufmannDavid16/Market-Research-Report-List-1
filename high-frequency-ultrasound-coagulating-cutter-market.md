<p><ul><li>Monopolar</li><li>Bipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Frequency Ultrasound Coagulating Cutter market is divided into two main types: monopolar and bipolar devices. Monopolar cutters use a single electrode to deliver energy through the tissue, allowing for precise cutting and coagulation but requiring a grounding pad for patient safety. In contrast, bipolar cutters utilize two electrodes to deliver energy directly between them, minimizing damage to surrounding tissues and enhancing control during procedures. Both types are crucial in surgical applications for effective tissue management and reduced bleeding.</p></p>
