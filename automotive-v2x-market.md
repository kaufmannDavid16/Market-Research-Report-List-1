<p><p>The Automotive V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) market comprises two primary types: Dedicated Short-Range Communications (DSRC) and Cellular V2X (C-V2X). DSRC is a short-range, low-latency communication system specifically designed for vehicle safety and efficient traffic management, operating in the 5.9 GHz band. Conversely, Cellular V2X leverages existing cellular networks, enabling broader coverage and integration with mobile technologies. Both technologies enhance connectivity between vehicles, infrastructure, and pedestrians, improving safety, traffic flow, and enabling advanced applications in smart transportation.</p></p>
