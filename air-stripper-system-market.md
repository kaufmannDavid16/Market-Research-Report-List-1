<p><strong>The Air Stripper System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sieve Tray System</li><li>Packed Tower System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air stripper system market primarily includes two types: Sieve Tray Systems and Packed Tower Systems. Sieve Tray Systems use perforated trays to facilitate gas-liquid contact, promoting the removal of volatile compounds from water. Their design enhances efficiency but may require more maintenance. Packed Tower Systems, on the other hand, utilize packing materials to increase surface area and improve contact between air and liquid, achieving effective mass transfer. Both systems cater to diverse industrial applications for water treatment and environmental remediation.</p></p>
