<p><p>The mobile transfer chairs market comprises two key types: manual and electric mobile transfer chairs. In contrast, electric mobile transfer chairs are powered by batteries, offering enhanced ease of use and minimizing physical strain on caregivers. These electric models are ideal for patients with limited mobility, promoting more efficient transfers and increasing comfort during transportation.</p></p>
