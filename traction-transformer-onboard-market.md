<p><p>The Traction Transformer is a critical component in electric traction systems, converting high voltage power to suitable levels for trains and electric vehicles. It operates in two primary market types: AC and DC. AC traction transformers are used in systems powered by alternating current, offering advantages like efficiency and ease of voltage transformation. Conversely, DC traction transformers serve direct current systems, providing stability for applications like metros and trams, where consistent performance is vital for safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
