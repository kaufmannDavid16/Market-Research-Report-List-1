<p><strong>The Flexible Copper-Clad Laminate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tape Casting</li><li>Sputtering</li><li>Electroplating</li><li>Laminating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flexible Copper-Clad Laminate (FCCL) market encompasses various manufacturing techniques. Tape casting involves applying a slurry of copper onto a substrate, forming a thin layer upon drying. Sputtering is a vacuum-based deposition method where copper atoms are ejected from a target and deposited onto the laminate. Electroplating involves coating a substrate with copper through an electrochemical process, ensuring uniform thickness. Laminating combines layers under heat and pressure to structure the material, enhancing flexibility and performance in electronic applications.</p></p>
