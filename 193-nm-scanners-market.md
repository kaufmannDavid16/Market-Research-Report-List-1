<p><p>The 193 nm scanner market is divided into two main types: dry scanners and wet scanners. 193 nm dry scanners utilize a dry photoresist process, offering simplicity and faster throughput, making them suitable for high-volume production. In contrast, 193 nm wet scanners employ a wet photoresist process, which enhances resolution and depth of focus, beneficial for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. Each type caters to different applications in lithography, impacting overall efficiency, precision, and production costs in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
