<p><ul><li>Lithium Ion (Li-ion) Battery</li><li>Nickel Cadmium (Ni-Cd) Battery</li><li>Nickel Metal Hydride (Nimh) Battery</li><li>Alkaline-Manganese Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The respirator medical batteries market includes several key types. Lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries are favored for their high energy density and lighter weight, providing longer usage times. Nickel Cadmium (Ni-Cd) batteries are known for their durability and ability to perform in extreme conditions but suffer from memory effect. Nickel Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries offer a balance of capacity and cost, with lower toxicity. Alkaline-Manganese batteries, while less common in medical devices, are reliable for non-rechargeable applications thanks to their affordability and availability.</p></p>
