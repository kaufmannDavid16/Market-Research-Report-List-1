<p><strong>The IR Window Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystal Material</li><li>Polymer Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IR window market is categorized into two primary types: crystal materials and polymer materials. Crystal material IR windows, made from substances like germanium and sapphire, offer superior optical clarity and durability, making them ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, polymer material IR windows, typically composed of materials like polyethylene or polycarbonate, are lightweight and cost-effective, suited for applications where flexibility and lower costs are essential. Each type serves unique needs across industries such as aerospace, defense, and medical technologies.</p></p>
