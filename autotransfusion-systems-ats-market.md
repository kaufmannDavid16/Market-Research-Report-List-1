<p><p>Autotransfusion Systems (ATS) are medical devices designed to collect, process, and reinfuse a patient's own blood during surgery or trauma. The market is segmented into two types: Continuous Autotransfusion Systems, which offer constant blood collection and transfusion during procedures, ensuring minimal blood loss and optimal recovery; and Discontinuous Autotransfusion Systems, which collect blood at intervals for reinfusion later, making them suitable for less complex surgeries. Both systems enhance patient safety and reduce the risk of transfusion-related complications.</p></p>
