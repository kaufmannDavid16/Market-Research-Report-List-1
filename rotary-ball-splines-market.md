<p><p>Rotary Ball Splines are precision mechanical components that allow rotational motion while enabling linear movement along a shaft. The market is primarily divided into two types: SPB and SPR. SPB (Straight ball spline) offers a straightforward design for applications requiring linear motion with minimal friction, while SPR (Raceway ball spline) features a more complex design that provides higher load capacity and rigidity. Both types cater to various industries, including robotics, automation, and CNC machinery, enhancing performance and efficiency in linear and rotational movements.</p></p>
