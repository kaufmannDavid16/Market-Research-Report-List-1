<p><strong>The Concrete Bonding Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cementitious latex based</li><li>Epoxy based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete bonding agents are essential for enhancing adhesion between surfaces. Cementitious latex-based bonding agents combine latex polymers with cement to improve flexibility and water resistance, making them ideal for interior and exterior applications. In contrast, epoxy-based bonding agents consist of epoxy resins providing exceptional strength and durability. They are often used in demanding environments, such as heavy-duty repairs and industrial settings. Each type serves specific needs, ensuring optimal performance in various concrete construction and repair projects.</p></p>
