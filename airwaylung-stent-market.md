<p><p>The airway/lung stent market is categorized into four main types: metal, silicone, hybrid, and others. Metal stents, usually made from stainless steel or titanium, offer durability and support in airway management. Silicone stents are flexible, biocompatible, and often used for temporary support with less risk of irritation. Hybrid stents combine the properties of both materials for enhanced flexibility and strength. Other stents may include biodegradable options or specialized designs tailored for specific clinical needs, expanding treatment options in airway obstruction cases.</p></p>
