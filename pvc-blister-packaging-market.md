<p><strong>The PVC Blister Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoforming</li><li>Cold Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC blister packaging market is primarily divided into two types: thermoforming and cold forming. Thermoforming involves heating plastic sheets to form hollow cavities, creating a secure and visually appealing packaging solution, typically for consumer goods and pharmaceuticals. Cold forming, on the other hand, utilizes aluminum or multilayer materials to create a stronger, airtight barrier, enhancing product protection, particularly for sensitive medicines. Both methods cater to diverse industry needs, ensuring both functionality and product integrity.</p></p>
