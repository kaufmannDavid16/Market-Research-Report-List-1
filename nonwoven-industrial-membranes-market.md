<p><p>Nonwoven industrial membranes are categorized into several types based on their structure and application. Spiral wound membranes feature a coiled design, enhancing surface area and efficiency for filtration processes. Hollow fiber membranes consist of thin fibers creating numerous channels for water purification and gas separation. Tubular membranes utilize a cylindrical shape for optimal flow and separation. Other types encompass specialized designs tailored for specific applications, such as microfiltration or ultrafiltration, catering to diverse industrial needs in water treatment and separation technologies.</p></p>
