<p><p>A Bimetal Temperature Gauge is a mechanical device used to measure temperature, utilizing the principle of thermal expansion in two different metals. These metals, bonded together, expand at different rates when heated, causing the bimetallic strip to bend. This bending movement is translated to a dial or digital display, providing an accurate temperature reading. Bimetal temperature gauges are widely used in various industries, including HVAC, automotive, and manufacturing, due to their reliability, simplicity, and durability.</p><p>The Bimetal Temperature Gauge Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period. <p><p>The bimetal temperature gauge market is characterized by three main types: angle type, straight type, and adjustable angle type. Angle type gauges feature a dial positioned at a right angle to the sensing element, allowing for easy reading in confined spaces. Straight type gauges have a linear orientation, ideal for vertical installations. Adjustable angle type gauges offer flexibility by allowing the user to set the dial to the desired position, enhancing visibility and usability in various applications.</p></p>
