<p><p>The Geographic Information System (GIS) market encompasses several key types: GIS collectors, which gather and digitize spatial data; total stations that combine electronic theodolites and distance measuring tools for precise surveying; imaging sensors used for capturing high-resolution aerial or satellite images; GNSS/GPS antennas facilitating accurate positioning and navigation; and LIDAR technology that measures distances using laser light to create detailed 3D maps. Together, these components enable effective spatial analysis, mapping, and data management in various applications.</p></p>
