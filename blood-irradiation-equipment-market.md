<p><strong>The Blood Irradiation Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Blood Irradiation Equipment</li><li>Gamma-ray Blood Irradiation Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood irradiation equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: X-ray and gamma-ray blood irradiation equipment. X-ray blood irradiation utilizes high-energy X-rays to inactivate pathogens in blood products, enhancing safety for transfusions. In contrast, gamma-ray blood irradiation employs gamma radiation, typically sourced from isotopes like Cobalt-60, to achieve similar results. Both technologies aim to reduce transfusion-related infections by effectively sterilizing blood, ensuring better patient outcomes and meeting stringent safety standards in healthcare settings.</p></p>
