<p><p>The military unmanned underwater vehicles (UUV) market is primarily categorized into two types: Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROV) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUV). ROVs are tethered, remotely controlled by operators from the surface, primarily used for tasks like surveillance, inspection, and recovery. In contrast, AUVs are self-contained and operate independently, utilizing pre-programmed missions for data collection and reconnaissance. Both types serve critical roles in defense operations, enhancing capabilities in underwater exploration and maritime security.</p></p>
