<p><strong>The Trim System for Boats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trim Tabs</li><li>Interceptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The trim system for boats primarily includes trim tabs and interceptors. Trim tabs are adjustable surfaces mounted on the transom that can be angled to alter the boat's attitude in the water, improving stability and fuel efficiency. Interceptors, on the other hand, are fixed or movable devices that create lift by modifying the water flow over the hull, allowing for quicker response and improved performance at various speeds. Both systems enhance maneuverability and optimize the boating experience.</p></p>
