<p><p>The ETFE tubing market is primarily divided into two types: heat shrinkable tubing and extruded tubing. Heat shrinkable tubing is designed to contract when heated, providing a snug fit and effective insulation for various applications, while extruded tubing is manufactured through a continuous process, offering uniform thickness and strength. Both types are used for electrical insulation, corrosion protection, and chemical resistance in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction, showcasing their versatility in demanding environments.</p></p>
