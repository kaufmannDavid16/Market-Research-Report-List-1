<p><p>The Performance Polyolefins market includes various types such as Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE), Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE), and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE). LDPE is known for its flexibility and low strength, making it suitable for films and packaging. LLDPE offers improved tensile strength and elasticity, widely used in stretch films and containers. HDPE boasts high strength and rigidity, ideal for durable goods like bottles and containers. Other polyolefins include specialty grades that cater to specific applications in various industries.</p></p>
