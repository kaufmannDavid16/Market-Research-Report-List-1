<p><strong>The Run-Flat Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-supporting</li><li>Self-sealing</li><li>Auxiliary-supported</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The run-flat tyres market comprises three main types: self-supporting, self-sealing, and auxiliary-supported tyres. Self-supporting tyres feature reinforced sidewalls that maintain the tyre's shape even when deflated, allowing for safe driving at reduced speeds. Self-sealing tyres contain a special inner sealant that automatically repairs small punctures, minimizing air loss. Auxiliary-supported tyres rely on an additional support ring inside the tyre, providing stability when pressure drops. Each type enhances safety and convenience during punctures, catering to different consumer needs.</p></p>
