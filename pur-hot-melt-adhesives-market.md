<p><p>The PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives market is categorized into two main types: Non-Reactive and Reactive PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives. Non-Reactive PUR adhesives solidify through cooling without undergoing a chemical change, offering quick setting times and excellent bond strength for various applications. Reactive PUR adhesives, on the other hand, cure through a chemical process when exposed to moisture, providing enhanced durability and resistance to environmental factors. Both types are utilized across industries like automotive, packaging, and construction for their versatile bonding capabilities.</p></p>
