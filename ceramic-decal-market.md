<p><strong>The Ceramic Decal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Decals</li><li>Silkscreen Decals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic decal market is primarily categorized into two types: digital decals and silkscreen decals. Digital decals leverage advanced printing technology to create intricate designs directly onto ceramic surfaces, offering high customization and quick production times. In contrast, silkscreen decals involve a traditional process where ink is pushed through a stencil onto the ceramic, providing vibrant colors and durability. Both types serve various applications in decorative ceramics, tableware, and art, catering to diverse consumer preferences and market demands.</p></p>
