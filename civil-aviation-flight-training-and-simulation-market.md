<p><p>The Civil Aviation Flight Training and Simulation market is primarily divided into two main types: Full Flight Simulators (FFS) and Flight Training Devices (FTD). FFS are sophisticated, high-fidelity simulators that replicate the complete flight experience, providing realistic training for pilots, often including motion and visual systems. In contrast, FTDs are less complex and focus on specific flight tasks or procedures without full motion capabilities, serving as effective tools for training in aspects like systems management and standard operating procedures.</p></p>
