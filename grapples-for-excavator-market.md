<p><strong>The Grapples for Excavator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The excavator grapple market is divided into two main types: hydraulic and mechanical grapples. Hydraulic grapples utilize hydraulic power to open and close their jaws, offering precise control and the capability to handle heavier loads, making them ideal for demolition and material handling. In contrast, mechanical grapples rely on a simple mechanical mechanism, often operated manually or with limited hydraulic assistance. These are typically more cost-effective and suitable for lighter tasks, providing versatility in various excavation and construction applications.</p></p>
