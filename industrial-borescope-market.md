<p><p>The industrial borescope market is broadly categorized into two primary types: flexible borescopes and rigid borescopes. Flexible borescopes feature a pliable, elongated design that allows for navigating complex or confined spaces, making them ideal for inspecting intricate machinery or hard-to-reach areas. Rigid borescopes, on the other hand, have a fixed straight design, providing excellent image quality and precision in inspection tasks where accessibility is less of a concern. Both types are essential tools in various industries for maintenance and quality assurance.</p></p>
